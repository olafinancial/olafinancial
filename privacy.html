Privacy Policy

Effective: 22 July 2026 · Controller: Pul Planning (pul.llc) · Contact: privacy@pul.llc

Plain language: We collect the financial information you enter so the app can show dashboards, tax estimates, and reports. We do not sell your data. You can reset or delete your account from Settings. This policy is intended to align with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023 and GAID 2025. Have your counsel review before treating this as final legal advice.

1. Who we are

Pul Planning (“Pul”, “we”, “us”) operates the personal finance web application at https://pul.llc. We process personal data as a data controller for accounts we operate.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data: name, email, authentication credentials (handled by Supabase Auth), optional profile fields (age, retirement age, currency preference, employment status).
  • Financial data you enter: income, expenses, assets, liabilities, goals, insurance, emergency fund, snapshots, calculators inputs, and related notes.
  • Technical data: device/browser type, IP address (via hosting/auth providers), session tokens, basic usage needed to secure the service.
  • Preferences: email digest settings, currency, Takaful preference, and similar app settings.

We do not require you to link live bank accounts or process payments through Pul for the core planning tools.

3. Lawful basis & purposes (NDPA)

  • Contract / requested service: create your account, store and display your financial entries, generate reports and projections.
  • Consent: optional product emails (digests) where you opt in; marketing only if you explicitly opt in later.
  • Legitimate interests: security, fraud prevention, service reliability, product improvement using aggregated or de-identified signals where appropriate.
  • Legal obligation: respond to lawful requests and maintain records where required.

4. How we use your data

  • Authenticate you and keep your session secure (including idle timeout).
  • Power dashboards, tax estimators (NTA 2025), debt tools, retirement and FI calculators.
  • Send password-reset and (if enabled) digest emails.
  • Provide account tools: reset data, load demo data, delete account.

We do not sell personal data. We do not use your financial entries to trade securities on your behalf.

5. Processors & transfers

We use third-party infrastructure to run the product, including:

  • Supabase — authentication and PostgreSQL database (with row-level security so users only access their own rows).
  • GitHub Pages / CDN — static web hosting for the front end.
  • Email providers (e.g. Supabase Auth email / Resend) — transactional and digest mail when configured.
  • Optional application server (e.g. Render) — digests, account deletion, macro data APIs.

Some providers may process data outside Nigeria. Where cross-border transfer rules under NDPA apply, we will use appropriate safeguards (e.g. contractual protections) and update this policy as our hosting footprint changes. CBN data-localisation rules for payment data (from 2027) will be considered if we ever process regulated payment transactions.

6. Security

  • Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest via our backend provider.
  • Row-level security on financial tables.
  • Service-role keys kept only on servers, never in the browser.
  • Session idle timeout and strong password requirements at sign-up.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Report suspected breaches to privacy@pul.llc.

7. Retention

We keep your data while your account is active. If you delete your account (Settings → Delete account) or request erasure, we remove or anonymise personal data within a reasonable period, except where we must retain records for legal, security, or dispute purposes.

8. Your rights

Subject to NDPA and applicable law, you may have rights to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete data / close your account
  • Withdraw consent where processing is consent-based (e.g. digests)
  • Object or restrict certain processing
  • Lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC)

In-product: use Settings → Testing & account for data reset and account deletion. Or email privacy@pul.llc.

9. Children

Pul is intended for adults managing personal finances. We do not knowingly create accounts for children under 18.

10. Cookies & local storage

We use essential session storage and local preferences (currency, UI flags, service worker cache). These are required for the app to function; we do not run third-party advertising trackers in the core product.

11. Breach notification

Where required by NDPA, we will notify the NDPC within applicable timelines (including the 72-hour standard where it applies) and inform affected users without undue delay when a breach is likely to result in a high risk to their rights.

12. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance where permitted by law.

13. Contact

Privacy enquiries: privacy@pul.llc
